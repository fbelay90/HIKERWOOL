The Science of Merino

Why serious hikers choose wool over synthetics.

Temperature Regulation

Merino fibres naturally adapt to your body temperature — warm when it's cold, cooling when you heat up. No more layering guesswork.

Odour Resistance

Merino's antimicrobial properties inhibit odour-causing bacteria. Wear it for multiple days on the trail without the funk.

Ultimate Comfort

Unlike coarser wools, merino fibres are ultra-fine and soft against skin. No itching — just comfort across miles.

Naturally Sustainable

Wool is biodegradable, renewable, and carbon-storing. Our ZQ-certified merino comes from farms with the highest animal welfare standards.
